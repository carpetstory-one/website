# For Interior Designers

We work directly with architects, interior designers, and hospitality professionals to source, customize, and commission handmade rugs for specific projects.

Our trade program is designed to simplify the specification process, providing you with dedicated support from initial concept to final installation.

---

## Trade Benefits

As a registered trade partner, you receive:

- **Trade Pricing**: Exclusive discounts across our entire collection.
- **Customization**: Alter the size, scale, or color palette of any existing design to fit your project.
- **Bespoke Commissions**: Work with our design team in Jaipur to weave a completely new, one-of-one piece.
- **Samples**: Complimentary strike-offs and material samples shipped directly to your studio.
- **Dedicated Support**: A single point of contact to manage timelines, freight, and installation logistics.

## The Process

<SlideIn direction="u">
### 1. Specification
Share your floor plans, fabric swatches, or mood boards. We'll recommend pieces from the archive or begin drafting a custom design.
</SlideIn>

<SlideIn direction="u" delay={100}>
### 2. Strike-off
For custom orders, we dye the yarn and weave a small 2x2ft sample (a strike-off) for your approval before putting the full piece on the loom.
</SlideIn>

<SlideIn direction="u" delay={200}>
### 3. Production
Once approved, the rug is woven. Depending on the knot count and scale, this takes between 12 to 36 weeks. We provide regular visual updates from the loom.
</SlideIn>
